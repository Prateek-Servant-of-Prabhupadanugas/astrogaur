Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At Astro Gaur, we hold the privacy of your personal and cosmic data in the highest regard. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, and safeguard the birth information and personal credentials you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

To calculate accurate Vedic astrology charts, natal metrics, and planetary alignments, we collect specific astronomical and personal data when you reach out via WhatsApp or social channels:

  • Full Name and Contact Number (WhatsApp ID).
  • Exact Date of Birth.
  • Exact Time of Birth.
  • Exact City/Place of Birth.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your natal parameters are treated strictly as cryptographic inputs for calculations. We use this information solely to:

  • Construct and analyze your personalized Vedic astrological birth chart.
  • Deliver consulting directives, chart summaries, and reports directly to your WhatsApp.
  • Respond to your operational inquiries and customer support communications.

3. Data Confidentiality & Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your private birth data or contact info to third-party ad networks. All data processing occurs in private environments. Because chart analysis is conducted and communicated through WhatsApp, your text records are subject to WhatsApp's native end-to-end encryption protocols.

4. Third-Party Redirection Links

Our platform contains redirection features linking out to major social platforms (YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook). Once you click away from Astro Gaur, your data interactions are governed entirely by the terms and privacy frameworks of those specific platforms.

5. Updates to This Policy

Astro Gaur reserves the right to modify this structural privacy document at any point to align with modern web safety regulations. Any adjustments will reflect immediately on this page via an updated effective date timestamp.
