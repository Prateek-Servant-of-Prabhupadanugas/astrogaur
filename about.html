01. The Principle of the Macro-Clock

Think of a standard wristwatch. The gears inside your watch turn the hour and minute hands. When the hands point to 9:00 AM, you leave for work. Did the watch *force* you to leave your house? No. The watch does not exert a magical gravity pull that drags your body out the door. The watch simply **reflects a pre-existing alignment of time.**

The solar system operates exactly like a macro-clock. The planets are massive cosmic gears, moving at mathematically perfect speeds across specific geometric coordinates. Vedic Astrology doesn't claim a rock floating millions of miles away controls your brain. It proves that the positions of these cosmic gears map out the energetic conditions and cycles of our environment.
